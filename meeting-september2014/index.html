September 2014 Meeting

Dates

9–11 September, 2014

Location

Auchrannie Resort, Brodick, Isle of Arran

Transport

The resort can be reached by train and ferry from Glasgow; these timetables give transit from Glasgow Central to the island. Transportation from the ferry can be arranged by contacting the hotel directly. (If many of us take the same ferry then we can coordinate to arrange transport to the hotel together.)

Tickets from Edinburgh, Glasgow Central, or Glasgow Airport to Brodick (including the ferry) can be booked online.

We expect most attendees will take the 1230 ferry on the 9th and eat lunch on board (you can bring your own food, or eat at the onboard cafe / restaurant).

Schedule

Start: 1400, Tuesday 9th September. End: 1200, Thursday 11th September. Details: TBA.
